Privacy Policy

Whether · Last updated 20 June 2026

The short version: Whether has no user accounts and no servers of its own. Your information stays on your device or is handled directly by Apple — we never collect it.

Whether (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is an iOS app published by Whether. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information the app uses

Location. With your permission, the app uses your device’s location while you are using it to fetch a local forecast for where you are. Your location is sent to Apple’s Weather service (WeatherKit) to retrieve weather data, and is used on your device to show your area’s name. We do not store your location, and we have no server that could receive it. You can turn location access on or off at any time in iOS Settings › Privacy & Security › Location Services.

Weather data. Forecasts are provided by Apple Weather (WeatherKit). Apple’s handling of any data involved in serving the forecast is governed by Apple’s privacy policy.

Purchases. “Whether. Plus” subscriptions are sold and processed by Apple through the App Store. All payment is handled by Apple — we never see or receive your payment details. The app only checks, on your device, whether you currently have an active subscription.

Your preferences. Your comfort settings and app preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS’s standard storage. They may be included in your personal device backups (for example iCloud or computer backups), which are controlled by you and Apple. They are not sent to us.

What the app does not do

  • No accounts, sign-in, or profiles.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising.
  • No third-party SDKs that collect your data.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information — there is nothing for us to share.

Third-party services

The app relies on Apple services — Apple Weather (WeatherKit) for forecasts and the App Store (StoreKit) for subscriptions. Your use of these is also covered by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data lives on your device, deleting the app removes its locally stored preferences. You can revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings, and manage or cancel a subscription in the App Store (Settings › your name › Subscriptions).

Children’s privacy

Whether is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
